WORK INFO

IMAGE LANGUAGES

2023
176 mm × 250 mm (each page)
Hand Collages

In this work, influenced by the deconstructivist Edward Fella, who embraces subconscious forms, I aimed to focus on my own unconscious design with a goal of achieving a sense of subconscious sensation and visual harmony.

I concentrated on my own design by adopting a fundamental approach like cutting and combining to discover my unique design language and find my own colors.

Through collage and image extraction, I explored how to compose a given screen with keywords such as color, composition, harmony, and restraint.
