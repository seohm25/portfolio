WORK INFO

CITY PEOPLE


2020
909 mm × 727 mm, 651 mm × 530 mm,
530 mm × 455 mm, 318 mm × 318 mm
Oil paint on canvas



'City people' series are about the momentary images of people in the city. When I see people coming and going in and out of the city, they have their own facial expressions. Sometimes when I look at them, I often find it interesting to see them staying in their personal thoughts.

I personally think their appearance is a connection that stimulates curiosity about their thoughts or concerns and makes them imagine various stories in a scene.
