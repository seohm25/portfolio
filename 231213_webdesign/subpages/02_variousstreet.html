A VARIATION OF THE STREET




N/A
Digital drawing



The stop-motion film titled "A Variation on the Street" documents instances of transformation happening in both urban and natural environments.

My viewpoint, marked by uncertainty about my direction, is portrayed as a dynamic presence bustling within the surroundings. Individuals persisting through their unique challenges and the ever-evolving nature are integral components of the natural rhythm in city life. As the video advances towards its later stages, various shapes intersect, and alterations in size and motion intensify, symbolizing the fusion of the city, its residents, and nature.

This underscores the dynamic movement within the city that encompasses the fusion of the city, its residents, and natures both the natural world and humanity.
