WORK INFO

UTOPIA

2020
909 mm × 727 mm
Oil paint on canvas
Group Exhibition in Ewha Art Center



During COVID-19 situation, this exhibition held in the Ewha Art center talked about the ideals each thinks under the theme of utopia.

At that time, the ideal surrounding for me was a peaceful environment with no disputes or competition. Based on my personal experience of going through a fiercely competitive society, I painted about the ideals and reality I think.
