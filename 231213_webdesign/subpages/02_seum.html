WORK INFO

SEUM BROCHURE

2023
297 mm × 210 mm
Pigment ink print


This artwork is a brochure designed with the aim of effectively and intuitively delivering information about the SEUM Foundation, a specialized organization for child welfare and family support, specifically aimed at the children of incarcerated parents and their families.

It adopts a visually oriented layout to present the foundation's objectives and achievements to not only current sponsors but also future supporters in a comprehensible manner. Through warm color schemes and playful styles of illustrations, the activities of the SEUM Foundation are meant to express a nurturing and caring approach towards children.
