WORK INFO

HAMIN SEO'S ATLAS

2023
176 mm × 250 mm (each drawing)
Color pencil, pen, graphite pencil, magazine, etc.


This work was influenced by Gerhard Richter's Atlas work. The Atlas, a 800 piece-archive formatted work made by reorganizing approximately 15,000 photographs, sketches, illustrations, etc., which are traces based on his memories.

‘Atlas’ showcases not only Richter's experiences but also his ideas and work processes. Through the work, he allows viewers to come into his world by understanding the logic in his visual thoughts, and flow of his life.

My ‘Atlas’ reveals my inherent self through daily drawings which is an important visual archive for my memories and experiences.
The reading gesture of flipping the large sketch-collection pages from left to right reflects the traditional reading method of the East, which speaks for my Korean identity.
