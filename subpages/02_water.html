A DIVERSE INNER WORLD

2020
455 mm × 379 mm, 258 mm × 179 mm
Acrylic paint on canvas, Conte on panel

Water is ‘the fundamental destiny that constantly transforms the substance of existence.” wrote Gaston Bachelard. This work is initiated from reading his book, ‘Water and Dreams.’

My interest in the book was the various forms and characteristics of water and the natural power of humans and basic substances. Visualizing water which is never constant in one shape, I tried to understand the somewhat ephemeral state of this substance. Within their infinite potential for change, I imagine diverse individual expressions reflecting the diversity of forms.
