WORK INFO

SIMILAR BUT DIFFERENT

2021
180 mm × 130 mm (each panel)
Ink pen on panel

My drawings reflect my interest in diversity and discrimination, intertwined with nature. Despite being different types of tree barks, they are the same trees. I wanted to deliver the idea of "similar but different" as something natural and inherent.

I structured each drawing in a linear arrangement format, encouraging the viewer to see them as different up close but as the same, one piece of art when viewed from a distance.
