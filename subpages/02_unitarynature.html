WORK INFO

UNITARY NATURE

2021
128 mm × 182 mm
Charcoal on paper

Despite our different appearances, we are all human beings. My work explores this notion by repeatedly generating various forms within the trees, coming together and dispersing, and implementing a form of chaos,I indirectly sought to reveal a hybrid identity.

Inspired by the artistic characteristics of William Kentridge, I used one piece of paper for this work as I repeated the drawing and erasing. I used stop-motion drawing with the fast and rough features of charcoal to focus on the flow of change. The drawings in the video, overlapping, disappearing, and coming together again, express "difference" and "diversity" while also revealing the complexity that occurs within them.
