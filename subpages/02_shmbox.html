Paper, Portable LED light


Through a shadow, I become a much bigger person. First I began to study letterforms so I used my initials, S, H, M, to create a wordmark and further developed into a paper structure. Then, I played with light on this structure and saw a resemblance of myself being adaptable to my surroundings. At the same time, the cube-like structure has characteristics of my determined and stubborn tendencies.

Light penetrating through the paper planes and shadows projecting onto the structure’s surroundings introduced new abstracted visual images. I use my hands to study and understand the world around me and my eyes are treated with unpredictable images which allow me to be creative beyond what I have already been accustomed to.
