WORK INFO

THE INNER WORLD

2019
1445 mm × 1121 mm, 1168 mm × 910 mm
Oil paint and ink pen on canvas



Each person has various thoughts and values according to their environment or experience, and accordingly, each has their unique inner vessel. I have abstractly visualized the various inner aspects of people in our society. Each invisible thoughts and emotions collide and mix in relationships in various societies.

Inspired by the surrealist painting style containing the unconscious flow, I was interested and curious to visualize what seemed invisible. Rather than drawing a specific object realistically, I worked as it flows.
