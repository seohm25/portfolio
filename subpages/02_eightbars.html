EIGHT BARS

2023
N/A
Paper


What if I were to design 'myself' graphically? These eight bars begin with 'Hamin Seo.'

Based on my initials 'SHM,' I created eight bars of the same size and shape.
Then, I made eight rectangles out of paper and continued experimenting by tossing and combining them, discovering irregular patterns that emerged when they scattered or came together. I captured this with photographs and created another form.This constant generation of new images implies the infinite potential I possess.

In the world of motion graphics, I discovered my true self within a dynamic transformation where eight bars come together and change fluidly. These elements, which can exhibit straight-line forms while also transforming into curves, show remarkable flexibility. This flexibility showcases my diverse talents while also hinting at my communication skills and adaptability.

This project, emphasizing the theme of endless transformation to highlight my potential, is an introduction of myself in the form of a mobile application.
The video experiments and motion graphics created with the eight bars will be shown in the app, providing users with visual intrigue. The ongoing image changes represent my boundless growth, and the rapid movements of the eight bars will leave a strong impression on the users, creating a vivid depiction of myself.
